Key Elements: Risk Identification: Identifying potential risks, both positive and negative, that could affect the project or organization. Risk Assessment: Evaluating the likelihood and potential impact of each identified risk. Risk Response Planning: Developing strategies to address the identified risks, including mitigation, avoidance, transfer, or acceptance. Risk Monitoring and Control: Continuously monitoring the risks and implementing controls to ensure the effectiveness of the risk management plan.
