The stakeholder engagement plan is designed to outline how decisions are made, who is responsible, and the appropriate level of involvement for each stakeholder. It provides a framework for decision-making that ensures the right stakeholders are involved, based on their roles, influence, and responsibilities in the project. By following the stakeholder engagement plan, the project manager ensures that the decision is made in alignment with the agreed-upon governance and with the appropriate input from relevant stakeholders, ensuring a balanced and informed decision.
