The project manager should **C. Start with a pilot project of appropriate complexity, and provide agile training to those who are impacted**. Starting with a pilot project allows the team to gain practical experience with agile methodologies in a controlled environment. This can help to identify potential challenges and solutions before rolling out the agile approach on a larger scale. Providing training to those impacted ensures that everyone has the necessary knowledge and skills to work effectively in an agile environment. This approach can also help to build buy-in from team members and stakeholders as they see the benefits of agile firsthand.
