According to PMI-PMP guidelines, to improve collaboration and address the issues faced by Team A, the project manager should take steps to enhance communication and ensure all teams feel included and valued. The most appropriate actions are: A. Update the communications management plan and encourage the teams to review it promptly and proactively. Reason: Updating the communications management plan ensures that it addresses the current communication challenges. Encouraging proactive review helps all teams understand their roles and the importance of timely and inclusive communication. B. Schedule weekly progress meetings with all three teams and have them take turns as key players. Reason: Weekly progress meetings with rotating key players ensure that all teams have an equal opportunity to voice their concerns and contribute. This helps in fostering a sense of inclusion and collaboration among all teams.

Selected Answer: AC
By updating the communications management plan, the project manager can establish clearer guidelines and processes for communication across different time zones and teams. This updated plan should outline expectations for communication frequency, methods, and protocols for sharing information. Encouraging all teams to review and adhere to this plan ensures that everyone is on the same page regarding communication practices, which can help address the issue of team A feeling left out due to delayed or missed information. Also, implementing a standardized status reporting template ensures consistent and timely updates from all teams. This template should include key progress updates, challenges, and any other relevant information that needs to be shared regularly among the teams. By sharing the template before the end of the day, team members can provide updates that will be ready and accessible for other teams when they begin their workday.
