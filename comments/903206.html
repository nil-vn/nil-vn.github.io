D. Change Request. A Change Request is a formal proposal to modify any document, deliverable, or baseline in a project. In this case, the inability of the supplier to deliver as per the original plan constitutes a change to the project's scope or schedule, necessitating a change request. This document will outline the specifics of the change (in this case, the delay or unavailability of materials), the impact on the project, and proposed alternatives or adjustments. It is then typically reviewed and approved by the project's change control board or similar authority. Issue Log: An issue log is used to record and track issues that arise during the project. While the supplier problem could be recorded here, the issue log itself is not usually used for formal communication of changes.
