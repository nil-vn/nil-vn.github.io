When facing a delay in a key component delivered by a third-party vendor, it's essential for the project manager to assess the impact on the project schedule and communicate this information to relevant stakeholders. This includes identifying and managing expectations, exploring possible mitigation strategies, and keeping all involved parties informed about the situation. Understanding the implications of the delay and communicating effectively contribute to proactive risk management and stakeholder engagement.
