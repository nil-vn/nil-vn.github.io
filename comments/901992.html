Role of the Project Manager According to the PMP - When an error impacts quality and team dynamics, the project manager must: * Analyze the causes → prevent it from happening again. * Support the team in improving → servant leadership. - Stakeholders influence the what (vision, objectives), but the team and the project manager manage the how (process, quality, dynamics). This is why A is preferred → direct action to improve the situation and the process.
