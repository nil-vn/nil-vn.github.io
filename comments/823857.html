The project manager should facilitate a discussion with the team to identify the root cause of the disagreement and work with the team to find a solution that works for everyone. This is consistent with the agile principle of self-organizing teams. Some potential solutions could include rotating meeting times, allowing team members to attend the meeting remotely, or finding an alternate communication method for team members who cannot attend the daily meetings. Establishing a team charter could be a helpful step in facilitating this discussion and ensuring that the team is aligned on their goals and values
