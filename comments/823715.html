Development team - Take responsibility for removing blockers that affect the team’s productivity. In PMP, the team is often empowered to identify and remove impediments to ensure smooth progress toward project objectives. Project manager/scrum master - Determine how to organize and carry out work to complete the minimum viable product (MVP). The project manager is responsible for planning, executing, and managing the project tasks to deliver the MVP on time and within scope. Product owner - Determine the version of the product that can be released to the market. The product owner’s role includes defining the project scope, prioritizing features, and making key decisions about what constitutes the final product that aligns with market needs.
