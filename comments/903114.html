According to PMI-PMP, when a software delivery project encounters technical impediments that may cause a delay in deliverables, the senior project manager should take the following action: C. Update the issue log with the status and priority. Updating the issue log with the status and priority will help the senior project manager keep track of the issue and its progress. It will also help the team to prioritize the issue and allocate resources accordingly. Sending a gentle reminder or issuing a formal warning may not be effective in resolving the issue, and using interpersonal skills to expedite the issue may not be appropriate in this situation.
