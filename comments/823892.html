PMBOK 7th Edition emphasizes Principle-Based Project Management, focusing on value delivery, stakeholder engagement, and risk management. Principle of Stakeholder Engagement The project sponsor is a key stakeholder who controls financial decisions. Engaging the sponsor ensures strategic alignment and that resources are secured. Principle of Value Delivery If the subcontractor cannot provide the same resources, value delivery is at risk. Escalating ensures that alternative solutions are explored before delays impact the project. Principle of Risk Management A cash flow problem is a significant risk that affects project execution. Escalating to the sponsor enables corrective action, like: Securing additional budget. Negotiating contract changes. Adjusting scope and schedule.
