In a situation where the project team is facing challenges, mistakes, and delays, it's crucial for the project manager to engage with the team to identify and address the issues. Open communication allows the team to discuss the challenges, share insights, and collaboratively find solutions to meet the project objectives. This approach fosters a sense of ownership and responsibility among team members, leading to improved collaboration and problem-solving. While team-building activities, bonus adjustments, and reminders of ground rules can be beneficial, initiating a discussion on overcoming challenges and meeting project objectives directly addresses the root causes of the issues and involves the team in finding effective solutions.
