It's important for the project manager to understand the reason behind the team lead issuing status updates independently. By having a discussion with the team lead, the project manager can gain insights into the motivation behind this action. It could be that the team lead felt the need to provide additional transparency or address specific concerns. Once the project manager understands the rationale, they can assess whether adjustments are needed to align communication efforts across all teams. This approach promotes collaboration and helps in addressing concerns raised by stakeholders.
