If the team is disengaged, the project manager should take steps to address the issue and improve team motivation. One way to do this is by scheduling one-to-one meetings with each team member to discuss their concerns, challenges, and goals. Additionally, scheduling team-building meetings can help improve collaboration, communication, and overall team morale. This can include virtual team-building activities or social events that allow team members to get to know each other better and build relationships outside of work.
