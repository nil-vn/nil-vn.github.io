In a situation where there is a lack of resources to deliver the project, the project manager should first identify the resource requirements and communicate this to the stakeholders. This involves clearly specifying the types and quantities of resources needed for the project. Once the resource requirements are communicated, the project manager can work with stakeholders to secure the necessary resources or explore alternative solutions. It's important to address resource constraints proactively to minimize the impact on the project schedule and costs.
