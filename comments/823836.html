The project manager should choose B. Hybrid approach for this project. A hybrid approach combines elements of both predictive (or traditional) and agile methodologies. The research part of the project, which is well-defined and can be planned out in detail, can be managed using a predictive approach. Once the research is completed, the delivery of the complex equipment, which may require flexibility and adaptability due to its complexity, can be managed using an agile approach. This allows for incremental delivery and the ability to make changes as needed based on customer feedback and changing requirements.
