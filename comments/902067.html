Fast-tracking involves overlapping activities that would normally be performed sequentially to shorten the overall project duration. In this scenario, the project manager can fast-track the activities by identifying tasks that have dependencies and limitations and then overlapping or parallelizing them to compress the schedule. This approach allows for achieving the customer's request to complete the project earlier by optimizing the sequence of activities and potentially running them in parallel.
