Selected Answer: D
D. Evaluate the suppliers based on their experience and skills in similar projects. Here's why: Objectivity: While former suppliers might have worked with the company previously, considering only their prior experience (option A) can lead to bias and limit options. Similarly, prioritizing the sponsor's recommendation (option C) might not guarantee the best fit for the project. **Contracting area involvement (option B) can be helpful for administrative tasks, but the project manager ultimately bears responsibility for supplier selection. Focusing on project needs: Evaluating suppliers based on their specific experience and skills in similar projects (option D) ensures a more objective and project-focused approach. This ensures you select a supplier who can deliver the required expertise and understanding of the project's context
