In an Agile project, collaboration and communication are crucial. If the product owner is consistently focusing on budget rather than product increments during ceremonies, it suggests a misalignment with Agile principles. The project manager should work with the product owner to clarify their role and expectations in an Agile project. This includes emphasizing the value-driven nature of Agile development, where the focus is on delivering valuable product increments rather than just tracking budget. The communication and collaboration plan should be adjusted to ensure everyone understands their roles and the purpose of Agile ceremonies.
