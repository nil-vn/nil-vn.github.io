Transitioning a project to an operational state involves ensuring that all deliverables have been completed and meet the required standards. Reviewing the work breakdown structure (WBS) with the project team is a good practice to confirm that all planned deliverables have been produced and validated. This step helps ensure that the project is ready for operational use and that there are no outstanding tasks or incomplete work that could affect the transition. It also provides an opportunity to verify that the project has met its objectives and delivered the intended outcomes.
