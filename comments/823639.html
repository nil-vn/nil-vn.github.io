C. Have a discussion with the team on the approach and come to a decision on when the daily standups should be. In agile, decisions regarding team practices and ceremonies, such as the timing of daily standups, should ideally be made collaboratively by the team. The project lead should facilitate a discussion with the entire team, including the agile coach and technical writer, to find a suitable compromise and agree on the timing of the daily standup that works for everyone. This approach encourages team ownership of their processes and fosters a sense of collaboration and commitment among team members. It's important to reach a consensus that respects the needs and constraints of all team members, especially in a globally distributed team.
