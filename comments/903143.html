D. Consult the resource management plan and escalate to the sponsor (recommended): This is the most comprehensive approach. Here's why: Resource management plan: Reviewing the plan allows assessing whether alternative resources within the project team or organization can fill the temporary gap. Escalation to sponsor: If internal resources are unavailable, informing the sponsor early allows them to explore options like: Negotiating with the marketing team: The sponsor might have leverage to convince the marketing manager to prioritize the project even with limited resources. Hiring temporary staff: Depending on the budget and urgency, temporary replacements could be hired for the key missing roles. Adjusting the project schedule: Modifying the timeline with realistic milestones can be considered if necessary.
