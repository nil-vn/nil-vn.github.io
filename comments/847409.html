Conducting meetings is a common technique for verifying knowledge transfer. In this context, the project manager can organize a meeting with relevant stakeholders, including members of the operations team, to confirm that the necessary knowledge has been transferred successfully. This provides an opportunity for team members to discuss and address any remaining questions or concerns and ensures that there is a shared understanding of the project's deliverables and the ongoing support requirements.
