The main point in this question is about the conflict, customer says the deliverables are not of quality and the project manager is certain they meet quality standards. Most likely reason is the customer is not aware of or overlooked the acceptable tolerance or variability. For example, if the material has to be 1 mm thickness, and it is 0.95 mm the customer may say it doesnt meet quality whereas project manager knows that 5% variability range should be accepted as per agreement. In this case it is about showing the customer the tolerance and control limits
