The project manager should choose option B. Restate to the developers the importance of focusing and meeting the sprint goal. While it’s important to address technical debt and potential issues, these should ideally be planned for and included in the sprint backlog during the sprint planning meeting. Remember, the key to successful Scrum is transparency, inspection, and adaptation.
