In an agile project, the product backlog outlines the features, enhancements, and fixes that need to be developed, while the definition of done (DoD) sets the criteria for when a product backlog item is considered complete. Understanding the impact of compliance on the project involves aligning these agile artifacts with the regulatory requirements. The product backlog helps in detailing the work to be done, including any compliance-related tasks, and the DoD ensures that the completed work meets the necessary standards, including compliance requirements. This combination allows the project manager to manage the development work in alignment with industry regulations.
