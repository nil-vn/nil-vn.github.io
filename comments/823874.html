The project manager should segment the types of communications to be delivered to different stakeholders based on their interest and need for information. Not all stakeholders require the same level of detail or frequency of updates. This approach will reduce the time spent by the project team in delivering several reporting formats for the same project updates to multiple stakeholders, while still ensuring that all stakeholders are informed and engaged appropriately. It is important to update the communications management plan to reflect this new approach.
