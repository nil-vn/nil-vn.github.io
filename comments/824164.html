Selected Answer: B
Internal team members: These are the people who have been working on the project on a day-to-day basis. They will be able to provide valuable insight into the critical technical issue and the possible solutions. Project team members: These are the people who are responsible for the project's overall success. They will need to be involved in the decision-making process so that they can ensure that the solution does not impact the project's overall goals. Management: These are the people who are ultimately responsible for the project's success or failure. They will need to be involved in the decision-making process so that they can approve any changes to the project's scope or budget.
