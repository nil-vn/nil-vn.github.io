In this situation, the project manager should collaborate with the team to assess the impact of the key team member's absence and work together to reprioritize critical tasks. This may involve redistributing the workload among the remaining team members or adjusting the iteration plan to focus on the most crucial deliverables for the end-of-iteration release. Open communication with the team is essential to ensure everyone is aware of the challenges and can collectively address the situation to meet the release deadline.
