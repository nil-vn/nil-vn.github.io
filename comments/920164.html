Iteration planning (Sprint planning) occurs at the beginning of an iteration to define and commit the work to be completed in the upcoming iteration => Team decided to commit more than 10% capacity in the next iteration = Iteration planning. Iteration review (Sprint review) occurs at the end of an iteration to showcase the product increment and to gather feedback on the delivered work => Product owner receiving feedback to add new requirements (not necessarily to be performed in the next iteration) = Iteration review.
