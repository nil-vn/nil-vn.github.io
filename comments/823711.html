PMI Agile guide Section 5.1, Page # 50: States: GROUND rules: Such as one person talking in a meeting. So in this situation PM needs to look at one at Team Charter - Ground Rules. If Ground rules state than only one or two person supposed to talk and provide updates that means team is doing what they agree too. In that case PM can sit with team to amend Ground rules for active participation. Once amendment made after that PM can utilise round Robin checks. So answer A is correct.
