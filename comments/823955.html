By formally identifying this new stakeholder and analyzing their needs, the project manager can develop a targeted strategy to ensure the stakeholder receives the right information (goals, objectives, benefits, risks) in the right way. This is a strategic, forward-looking action that enhances project success by building a positive relationship and managing expectations from the start.
