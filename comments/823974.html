The product owner is responsible for prioritizing and making decisions about the product, including when to release it. In the agile approach, the concept of a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) acknowledges that some features may be missing in the initial release, but the focus is on delivering value to customers as soon as possible. It's essential to support the product owner's decision and foster collaboration and alignment with stakeholders, including the marketing vice president. The project manager should work on improving communication and understanding among the team, the product owner, and key stakeholders to ensure that expectations are managed, and there is a shared understanding of the product strategy and its gradual improvement.
