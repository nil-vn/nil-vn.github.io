The correct method for displaying the value of work performed to date to project stakeholders is: B. Burnup Chart Explanation: A Burnup Chart is a visual representation that shows the progress of work completed (value delivered) over time against the total scope. It is commonly used in agile projects to provide stakeholders with a clear view of the work performed to date and how much work remains. It helps in tracking performance and evaluating if the project is on track to meet its goals.
