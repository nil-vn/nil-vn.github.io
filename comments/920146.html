When a defect is identified that significantly impacts system performance and puts the delivery date at risk, it is crucial to develop a plan to address the issue. Planning risk responses involves identifying and evaluating potential options to mitigate or bypass the performance issue, ensuring that the project can continue to progress and meet its deadlines. This proactive step helps to manage the risk effectively and minimize its impact on the project's success.
