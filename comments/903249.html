In a situation where there is a transition from predictive stages to iterative/agile stages and the team encounters challenges such as low commitment, conflicts, and confusion, team-building activities and efforts to enhance collaboration can be beneficial. Team-building activities can help foster a positive team culture, improve communication, and address any issues that may be impacting morale. Coaching on agile processes may also be useful, but addressing team dynamics and collaboration is a key aspect of improving the team's performance in an agile environment.
