Maximizes Value: By releasing the product to each country as it is finalized, the team can start generating revenue sooner from each market. Feedback Loop: Early releases allow the team to gather feedback from initial markets, which can be used to improve the product for subsequent releases. Risk Mitigation: Any issues identified in the earlier releases can be addressed before launching in other countries, reducing the risk of widespread problems. Agile Principles: This approach aligns well with agile methodologies, which emphasize iterative development and continuous delivery.
