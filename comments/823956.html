The correct answer is: B. Implement plans to remove the obstacles. This keeps the project progressing and demonstrates leadership in problem-solving. Breakdown of other options: • A. Escalate to the project steering committee – Escalation is appropriate only if the project manager cannot resolve the obstacles. • D. Prioritize the obstacles for resolution – Prioritizing is helpful, but action is needed to remove them; prioritization alone does not solve the issue.
