B. Send the weekly report to the PMO and the product owner, and use a kanban board for the project team. In an agile delivery approach, communication is usually more frequent and informal than in traditional project management approaches. The project team and product owner typically use a visual management tool, such as a kanban board, to manage and communicate the status of the project. It is recommended to provide weekly reports to the PMO and the product owner to keep them updated on the project's progress. The daily standup is a meeting for the project team and the product owner to discuss the project's status, and stakeholders are not typically invited to attend.
