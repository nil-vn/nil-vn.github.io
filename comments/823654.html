When working with a new team on a project, the Agile Facilitator should start by holding a team charter discussion. This session helps the team establish a shared understanding of the project's purpose, goals, and expectations. During the session, the team will discuss the scope of the project, the stakeholders involved, and the team's roles and responsibilities. This discussion will help the team align on the project's objectives, establish a foundation of trust, and identify potential risks and challenges.
