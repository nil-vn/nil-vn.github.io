C. Have a high-level plan for incremental deliveries and get the customer's feedback as early as possible. By adopting an incremental delivery approach and seeking customer feedback early in the process, the project manager can obtain valuable input to guide the development and ensure alignment with the customer's requirements. This iterative approach enables early detection and resolution of any misunderstandings, potential issues, or changes in priorities, reducing the risk of rework or delays later in the project. This strategy capitalizes on the experienced project team, stakeholder support, and customer cooperation. It allows the project manager to leverage their expertise and foster collaboration between the project team and the customer throughout the development process, ensuring that the solution meets the customer's needs and expectations while maintaining a focus on timely delivery.
