When delays are caused by different interpretations of client requirements, the best approach is to use prototypes to: Visualize requirements clearly Align understanding between the client, project team, and development team Reduce the likelihood of further miscommunication and rework Prototyping is a powerful tool in software development, especially in cross-cultural or remote teams, where verbal or written descriptions may lead to different interpretations. It allows for early validation and helps ensure everyone is building the right product.
