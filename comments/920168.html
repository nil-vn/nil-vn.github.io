When senior management requests project information for decision-making, they need concise and high-level insights that provide a comprehensive overview of the project's progress and current status. The information should help them understand key achievements, critical points, and any potential issues or risks that might affect the project's success. A milestones status report provides a summary of the major achievements and critical points in the project timeline. Milestones represent significant events or deliverables, and their status can give senior management a clear view of the project's progress towards its goals. This report highlights whether the project is on track, behind schedule, or ahead, and identifies any key issues that might need management's attention.
