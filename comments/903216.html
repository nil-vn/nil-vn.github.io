When there is a significant regulatory change mid-project, the project manager must communicate with stakeholders in a clear, focused, and understandable manner. Stakeholders typically want to know: What has changed How it impacts the project (scope, schedule, cost, compliance) What decisions or actions are needed next A concise and simple report ensures the stakeholders quickly grasp the implications and can make informed decisions. Overly technical or lengthy reports may overwhelm or confuse stakeholders, especially executives or sponsors.
