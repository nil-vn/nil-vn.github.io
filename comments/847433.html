This information is crucial for devising effective strategies to address the conflict. Additionally, addressing the conflict during team meetings provides an opportunity for open communication and collaboration among the entire team, fostering a collective effort to find solutions and improve team dynamics. Combining individual discussions with broader team involvement helps create a more comprehensive and inclusive approach to conflict resolution.
