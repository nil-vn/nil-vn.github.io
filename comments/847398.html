C. Define a project management plan for the new approach, as needed. If the project board decides to replace Scrum with a predictive approach, the project manager should adapt the project management plan to reflect this change. This will involve updating the various components of the plan, such as scope, schedule, budget, and risk management, to accommodate the new approach. Additionally, the project manager should ensure that the team members and stakeholders are informed about the change and are aligned with the new methodology.
