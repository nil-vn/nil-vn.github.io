In a situation where a critical deliverable is causing difficulties, and the work is not well-coordinated among team members, the project manager should take actions to address the issues and improve team performance. Two appropriate actions are: C. Schedule an iteration backlog refinement meeting to understand the issues. By scheduling an iteration backlog refinement meeting, the project manager can gather the team to discuss and understand the issues that are affecting the current iteration. This meeting can help in identifying bottlenecks, clarifying requirements, and addressing any misunderstandings or coordination problems. D. Set up one-on-one meetings to provide feedback to the team members. Conducting one-on-one meetings with team members allows the project manager to provide personalized feedback, understand individual concerns, and address any performance issues. This can help in building a better understanding of the challenges faced by each team member and providing support where needed.
